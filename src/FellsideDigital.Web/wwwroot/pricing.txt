# Pricing — Fellside Digital

Modern websites and automation systems for small businesses that want more customers and
less admin. All prices in GBP. Every project is quoted at a fixed price agreed before work
begins. Setup = one-off; support/care = monthly. Ranges below are starting points; final
quote depends on scope.

## Websites (Astro static sites)

### Tier 1 — Static Marketing Site
- Setup: £295–£495 (one-off)
- Best for: sole traders, local services, simple portfolio/brochure sites
- Includes: fully static (no backend/logins), mobile-responsive design, basic SEO
  (meta tags, sitemap), contact form (Formspree or similar), fast Astro static build + deploy

### Tier 2 — Business Site with Light Logic
- Setup: £495–£1,750 (one-off)
- Best for: businesses needing booking, filtering, simple dynamic content or basic integrations
- Includes: everything in Tier 1, plus lightweight backend logic (availability checks,
  filtered listings, simple APIs), third-party integrations (Calendly, CRM hooks, tagged
  email capture), conversion-focused layout, Google Analytics 4, optimised enquiry flow

### Tier 3 — Advanced / Custom Build
- Setup: £1,750–£7,500+ (one-off)
- Best for: businesses needing custom systems, complex workflows or a web-app layer
- Includes: everything in Tier 2, plus complex business logic (multi-step forms, role-based
  content, dynamic pricing), database-backed or authenticated sections, custom integrations
  (bespoke APIs, ERP/CRM, data feeds), advanced SEO (service + location pages, schema),
  Core Web Vitals tuning, optional AI features (chatbots, smart forms, recommendations)

Most small businesses need Tier 1 or 2. Unsure? We scope it together before quoting.
